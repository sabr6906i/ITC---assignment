What We Do

The 'T' of IIT Bombay is right here

ITC is the apex body governing all technical activities at IIT Bombay. We connect students to resources, mentors, and platforms to build the next generation of innovators.

Support Ideas

From concept to prototype — ITC backs student ideas with funding, labs, and mentorship.

Connect Communities

Bridging 8+ technical clubs with a unified platform and shared resources.

Run Events

Hackathons, workshops, talks and competitions that keep the tech culture alive.
