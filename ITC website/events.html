Techfest — Asia's Largest S&T Festival

Techfest is Asia's largest science and technology festival, hosted annually at IIT Bombay. Co-organized with ITC, it brings together the brightest minds across the globe for competitions, exhibitions, lectures, and cultural exchanges spanning robotics, AI, aerospace, and more.

Every semester, ITC clubs open their doors to fresh talent. There's no formal application — just show up to sessions, participate, and let your enthusiasm speak. Announcements go out via webmail.

Tinkerer's Lab Open Sessions

The Tinkerer's Lab hosts open sessions where students can prototype their ideas using cutting-edge fabrication equipment — 3D printers, laser cutters, electronics benches — with peer and mentor support available throughout.
