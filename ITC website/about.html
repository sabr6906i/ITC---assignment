About ITC

The 'T' of
IIT Bombay

Institute Technical Council — the apex body for all technical activities at IIT Bombay, nurturing innovators since day one.

Our Mission

Why ITC Exists

Student tech is one of the pillars of IIT Bombay. We make sure your ideas are heard — and we help turn them into reality, every step of the way.

Nurture Innovation

Motivate young minds to pursue innovative ideas, no matter how ambitious. We provide the environment, tools, and community to help ideas flourish.

Connect Students

Bridge the gap between students and technical clubs, labs, and resources. ITC ensures no one with passion is left without a platform.

Build the Culture

Organize events, talks, competitions, and activities that keep the spirit of technology alive and thriving across the institute year-round.

Organisation

How ITC Is Structured

ITC operates under the Students' Gymkhana of IIT Bombay. It is headed by the General Secretary (Technical) and comprises conveners and managers across multiple clubs, tech teams, and bodies.

General Secretary (Technical)

Elected annually, leads the council, represents student tech interests to the institute administration.

Club Conveners & Managers

Each club has a convener and managers who organize events, drive recruitment, and mentor members throughout the year.

Web, Creatives & PG Teams

Cross-functional teams handle the council's digital presence, design assets, and postgraduate technical activities.

Other Bodies

DevCom, Anymation, CDS-SRG, and CSEC operate under ITC, broadening the technical landscape beyond traditional clubs.

Membership

How to Join

There is no formal way to join a club at ITC. The philosophy is open participation — just show up and get involved.

01

Watch Your Webmail

All club events, activities, and open sessions are announced via IIT Bombay webmail. Keep an eye out for invitations.

02

Attend & Participate

Walk into any club session or event that interests you. No signup, no gatekeeping — participation is all that matters.

03

Apply for the Council

Annual recruitments are held for formal positions like conveners, managers, and tech team members. Details shared on webmail.
